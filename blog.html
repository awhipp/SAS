Fall 2014 Wrap-Up

So, we last left off with me working a contract for a start up just outside of D.C. About 4 months later, I have completed the terms of my contract, and we have submitted my Google Glass application to Google for approval. That feedback just came to me, and I will begin revisions, and hopefully have my first Glassware application on the market around next semester! Interesting Turn of Events

September 04, 2014

So right now I am sitting on the plane returning from Arizona after a 5-day vacation from it all. Now what do I mean by "it all?" Well, this summer I decided to take a break from the normal "go to the beach and enjoy life" mentality. I buckled down and decided to take a 3-credit course in business management (for my recently added minor), and work 40-hour weeks in DC as a intern consultant at Booz Allen Hamilton. No big deal. The primary focus of my project was to integrate emerging technologies such as Google Glass, Oculus Rift, and cloud computing into a portable system for military use. All of this project, because of all of its developmental platforms, was used as a proof of concept to be later leveraged by anticipated contracts. It's honestly been a good summer, and only proved to me that anything can happen. Now off to get moved in to the University of Maryland and begin work at the Department of Justice. More to come...
